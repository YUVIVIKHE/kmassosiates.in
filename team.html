Who we are

Kiran Mohite And Associates (KMA) is a premier structural engineering consultancy founded by Kiran K. Mohite and Shubhangi K. Mohite. With over 25 years of expertise, we deliver innovative, reliable, and cost-effective engineering solutions across diverse sectors.

We stay involved from concept to site handover—optimizing systems for safety, economy, and buildability while keeping RFIs and coordination tight.
