Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What types of projects does KMA handle?

KMA specializes in residential high-rise towers, commercial buildings, industrial projects, and complex structures up to 250m height. We handle everything from preliminary design to construction support.

What software tools does KMA use?

We use industry-standard software including ETABS, STAAD-Pro, RCDC, SAFE, Draft-win, and AutoCAD for structural analysis, design, and detailing.

What codes and standards does KMA follow?

We strictly adhere to Indian Standards including IS 456-2000 (Concrete), IS 1893-2016 (Seismic), IS 16700-2023 (Tall Buildings), IS 13920-2016 (Ductile Detailing), and IS 875-1987 (Wind Loads).

Does KMA provide construction support services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive construction support including site visits, RFI responses, shop drawing reviews, and quality assurance throughout the construction phase.
