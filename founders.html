Meet the founders

Founder & Managing Director

Er. Kiran K. Mohite

Founder and Managing Director of the KMA, bringing a wealth of expertise in structural engineering and design leadership. He holds a Master’s degree in Structural Engineering (M.E. Structure) from Pune University and has successfully delivered projects across diverse sectors.

His portfolio spans:

  • Residential high-rise towers
  • Commercial complexes
  • Industrial plants / PEB Structures
  • Temporary and specialized structures

With a proven track record of managing complex projects, he combines technical precision with innovative design solutions. He is deeply committed to ensuring timely project delivery without compromising on quality—a principle that defines the firm’s ethos. Under his leadership, KMA continues to build a reputation for reliability, excellence, and forward-thinking design in structural engineering.

Co-founder & Director

Er. Shubhangi K. Mohite

Co-Founder and Director of KMA, completed Civil Engineering from the Government College of Engineering, Karad, and a recognized expert in the structural analysis and design of complex projects, including high rise residential and commercial towers reaching up to 250 meters.

With extensive experience across ETABS, SAFE, RCDC, Draft Win, and STAAD Pro, she brings precision and innovation to every project.

Her leadership ensures that designs are not only technically sound but also aligned with client objectives, thanks to her proven expertise in client coordination and project delivery.
