How we work

Lean, transparent workflow

1. Kickoff

Architectural review, risk register, data collection and programme alignment.

2. Modelling

ETABS/STAAD + SAFE models with iterative options for stability, drift and economy.

3. Detailing

Concrete detailing in RCDC/Draft-win + AutoCAD; coordinated with MEP shafts and cores.

4. Reviews

Internal QA/QC plus client/authority comments tracked to closure.

5. Construction

Site visits, RFIs, pour sequencing and shop review for constructability.

6. Handover

As-built updates (if required), final reports and digital archive.
